---
name: Understated tone preference
description: Jennifer prefers understated, secure confidence over self-promotion -- let the work speak, don't oversell
type: feedback
---

Keep all public-facing copy understated and confident. No self-promotion, no "look at me" energy. Jennifer knows her work speaks for itself and prefers a tone that feels secure rather than loud.

**Why:** She doesn't like a ton of publicity and finds overstated personal branding off-putting. Confidence should come through in what's stated, not how hard it's selling.

**How to apply:** When writing LinkedIn, website copy, or any public-facing text -- favor short, factual statements over persuasive language. Let metrics and outcomes land on their own without dressing them up. The website ticker approach (verb + fact) is the right energy.
